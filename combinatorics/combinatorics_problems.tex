\section{Problem 1, Bulgaria Round 3 Question 1, 1975}
Let $n$ be an odd natural number and $a_1$, $a_2$,..., $a_n$ a permutation of the numbers $1$, $2$,...,$n$. 

Show that the value $(a_1-1)*(a_2-2)*...(a_n-n)$ is an even number.
